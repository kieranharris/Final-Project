About Grigio

We are a band from "Gem City", bringing back the music scene in Dayton, Ohio. The band was created in fall 2018 and plays a variety of genres.

We play everything from rock and sludge metal, to jazz and alternative, and we have a full length album on all streaming services titled "Fine". Links will be found under "Music".

We are going on tour across the Mideast United States with local Dayton band Smoking Television. Lasting August 1-August 10, further information will be found under "Tour".

There will be many shows this summer 2019 before the tour begins, local and non-local with many talented artists. Dates will be added and found under "Shows".

So sign up on our website today by entering your email, and stay updated on all of Grigio's latest endeavors!
